My Ties to Colombia: I was born in Colombia and have lived in several regions, including Bogotá and Medellín.

Interesting Physical Feature: One fascinating feature of Colombia is the Andes Mountains, which split into three distinct ranges within the country and shape the climate and settlement patterns.

Spiritual Reflection: The beauty and complexity of Colombia's landscapes—its jungles, rivers, and mountains—testify to me of a Supreme Creator, as Alma 30:44 teaches. Nature’s harmony reveals divine purpose.
